**Voice Interaction Features Overview**

Voice interaction features in LISA are designed to provide users with a hands-free and intuitive way to interact with the assistant. By leveraging advanced speech recognition and text-to-speech technologies, LISA can understand and respond to voice commands, making it easier for users to manage tasks, access information, and control various functionalities.

**Recommended Techniques and Tools**

1. **Speech Recognition**
   - **Description:** Speech recognition technology converts spoken language into text, allowing LISA to understand and process voice commands.
   - **Tools:** Vosk, OpenAI Whisper, Google Speech-to-Text

2. **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**
   - **Description:** Text-to-speech technology converts text into spoken language, enabling LISA to provide voice responses to user queries.
   - **Tools:** pyttsx3, eSpeak, Google Text-to-Speech

3. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
   - **Description:** NLP techniques enable LISA to understand the context and intent behind voice commands, allowing for more accurate and relevant responses.
   - **Tools:** spaCy, HuggingFace Transformers, GPT-3

4. **Voice Command Customization**
   - **Description:** Users can customize voice commands to suit their preferences and needs, making the interaction with LISA more personalized and efficient.
   - **Tools:** Custom Python scripts, configuration files
